#### [JSON vs XML with Douglas Crockford](https://corecursive.com/json-vs-xml-douglas-crockford/)

Although the title may suggest a straightforward comparison between the advantages and disadvantages of JSON and XML, this podcast featuring Douglas Crockford, the creator of JSON, goes far beyond that. In it, Crockford recounts the history of JSON's inception and its successful challenge against XML, which was the dominant data format at the time. The podcast highlights how the quest for simplicity in software development, the fight against complexity, and the willingness to challenge prevailing ideas are still relevant and valuable to developers today.

#### [TCP over IP Anycast - Pipe dream or Reality?](https://engineering.linkedin.com/network-performance/tcp-over-ip-anycast-pipe-dream-or-reality)

This article provides a comprehensive explanation of how LinkedIn migrated from DNS-based load balancing to anycast-based for their primary domain (www.linkedin.com). While the idea of using an anycast IP for the domain and relying on BGP to choose the closest server seemed promising, the implementation faced several challenges. The article describes how a hybrid model, which combines DNS-based and anycast-based approaches, was ultimately adopted and resulted in maximum advantage for LinkedIn.
